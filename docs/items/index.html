Items

This is a list comprising of all items currently implemented in the mod.

Sabers

  • Krystal: Plasma exerting magical crystal.
  • Single Bladed Saber: An elegant weapon for a more civilised age.
  • Dual Bladed Saber: Melee weapon, excellent at parrying.
  • Rotary Saber: Melee AND ranged weapon. Also serves as temporary aerial escape.
  • Crossguard Saber: Weapon based off of ancient sith designs. Cracked krystal makes it blusterous.
  • Blaster Saber: Saber with modified blaster at core, mildly effective at ranged attacks.
  • Saber Pike: Another ancient sith weapon, later used by jedi guardians.
  • Saber Gauntlet: Hardcore melee weapon with brutal disarming capabilities. cancelled concept from ROTS.
