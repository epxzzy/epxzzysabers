Usage

Activating

To activate the item press [SHIFT + RMB]. Same can be done to turn it off. If a player throws (or fatfingers [Q]) an active saber from their mainhand it automatically turns off.

Attacking

When the item is active, it does 12hp of damage when left-clicked on an entity (or player). However like any other saber item, this can be countered via Blocking. A left-click can also deflect any incoming(in parry range, or in 3 block radius for this specific item) projectiles (given that the projectile is in a somewhat linear path, unlike shulkers for example), but timing would have to be precise.

Blocking

Rotary Saber, like any other saber item, when right-clicked, will block any damage coming from a saber item, and even projectiles if timed properly. Can be held down for about ~1.75 seconds which if exceeded will cause the projectile to do damage.

Boomrang

Pressing [SABER-ABILITY] key throws the rotary saber like a boomrang, once it hits an entity or a block it will return back to you. this will temporarly disable blocking after you pick it up. this however can be parryed and blocked. if the player is not in the distance (this also means not online, or in another dimention, or chunk not loaded) of the thrown rotary saber it will just crash into anything and drop as an item. Entites in the way of the thrown rotary saber will take repetitive 2hp damage, regardless of it returning back or not. Upon properly hitting an entity it does the same 12hp of damage as it would with meele, every other entity in path does take 2hp for being there.

Flight

Looking up, Jumping and holding down right-click will toggle flight as long as the item is held down. Flight is better suited for scaling vertically, like mountains for example, or if you just skill issue into the void. Flying does mean the user is vulnerable to any melee and ranged attacks. If a player is shot while flying they will stop flying. A flying player can only fly for 200 ticks (10 seconds) after which flight disables and they start falling. It will take them about [TO BE CHANGED] 40 ticks (2 seconds) to fly again if their flight has ended, canceled or disabled. Another Way to Counter a flying player is to activate Disruption.
