Getting Started

epxzzy's sabers adds a variety of laser and plasma weaponry to Minecraft, featuring advanced combat mechanics and animations.

Attacking

Every saber (items with the epxzzysabers:lightsaber tag) uses a randomized attack pool (1 of 8 animations) upon hitting an entity. This includes armor stands, item frames, and other interactable entities.

"Heavy weapons" like pikes and dual-bladed sabers are currently routed differently to allow for unique animation sets in future updates. Currently, attacking may cause an unintentional parry if a projectile is within range.

Blocking

Saber attacks can be blocked by holding down Right-Click, similar to a shield. Blocking only defends against other sabers (epxzzysabers:lightsaber tag) and does not defend against standard melee attacks.

However, a saber can block ANY projectile if held for less than 50 ticks (2.5 seconds). If this duration is exceeded, the block fails.

Note: You don't need to manually match attack directions; the saber automatically syncs with other players to block properly. Focus on facing the source of damage.

Parrying

Any arm swing (attacking, mining, or punching air) with a saber will perform a parry. A parry deflects any projectile within a certain range (determined by the saber's specific Parry Range) back in the direction the player is facing.

Note: Certain projectiles like shulker bullets or tridents may process differently and might be harder to redirect.

Parries feature three conditional animations based on player movement and orientation:

  • X-Cross Front: Default animation when standing still.
  • Spinning: Played when looking down (X-rotation > 40).
  • Grievous Move: Played while sprinting (Spinning has priority if looking down).
Stance menu

Assuming Stance

Crouching during a block triggers a Stance. There are eight total stances available, which can be configured at a KyberStation. Stances are primarily cosmetic but provide a unique visual identity to your combat style.

Available Stances:

  1. Shi-Cho: (Used by Luke Skywalker, Kit Fisto)
  2. Makashi: (Used by Count Dooku, Grand Inquisitor)
  3. Soresu: (Used by Obi-Wan Kenobi)
  4. Ataru: (Used by Qui-Gon Jinn, Master Yoda)
  5. Shien / Djem So: (Used by Anakin Skywalker, Ahsoka Tano)
  6. Niman: (Used by Darth Maul)
  7. Juyo / Vaapad: (Used by Mace Windu, Darth Sidious)
