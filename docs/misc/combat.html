Stancing

Press the [STANCE] key to assume a stance while crouching during blocking. Pressing SHIFT + [STANCE] opens the stance preference menu. Think of it as fighting with a highly specialized shield—your main concern is facing the source of damage.

Note: Critical hits are not applied to saber damage for balance purposes. A visual indicator displays when holding a saber in hanging block stance.
